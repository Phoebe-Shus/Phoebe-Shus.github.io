OUR MISSION

Warrior Wellness Winners is a concept we are testing for San Diego Start-Up weekend. Our concept is to offer a service that would educate veterans and active duty members to existing treatments that they may not know about and that can be combined with any traditional treatment they may already be receiving. We've learned that mixing the treatment modalities of traditional Western Medicine with Eastern Medicine can assist in faster and more effective recovery from many types of military related injuries. We'd love your feedback regarding weather or not you would be willing to try mixed method treatments and if you would pay a small membership due to support the service providers that offer the treatments and the counselor that will help you determine your best path, set appointments for you, follow up with your progress and make adjustments to your treatment plan as needed.
